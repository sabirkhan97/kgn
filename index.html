Our Story

For over a decade, KGN Hyderabadi Chicken Point has been serving authentic Hyderabadi cuisine to the people of Gurgaon. Located at the bustling Pataudi Chowk, we bring you the finest biryani, tandoori delights, and traditional chicken preparations that have made Hyderabad famous worldwide.

Each dish is crafted with premium ingredients, traditional spices, and a passion for excellence that you can taste in every bite. Our chefs follow age-old recipes while adding their unique touch to create an unforgettable dining experience.
